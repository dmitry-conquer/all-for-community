Tax Benefits for Donors

Your donation to All for Community is tax-deductible under IRS guidelines for 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations. By supporting our work, you not only help provide essential social services to individuals and families but also enjoy potential tax savings.

  • Individuals

    Charitable donations can generally be deducted up to 60% of your adjusted gross income (AGI), depending on the type of contribution.

  • Businesses

    Corporate donors can deduct up to 10% of their taxable income.

  • In-Kind Donations

    Non-cash contributions such as goods or services may also qualify for deductions, based on their fair market value.

Why Donate?

Your generosity directly impacts the lives of those in need, fostering dignity, equity, and support across our community. Plus, your contribution helps make a tangible difference while offering potential tax advantages.
